Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 June 2026. Version 2026-06-21.

Template notice. This is a starting-point policy for the test system and is not legal advice. Before any public launch, have it reviewed against UK GDPR / the Data Protection Act 2018, and fill in the controller details below.

1. Who we are

Meowtion ("we") is the data controller for the information described here. You can exercise your rights directly from your account page (download or delete your data); for anything else, use the contact details published with the project.

2. What we collect

  • Account: your email address (and, if you use Google sign-in, the basic profile Google returns).
  • Your cat's activity data: activity, sleep, posture, purring, eat/drink events, and battery level, plus the names you give your cats and stations.
  • Device identifiers: the IDs of the collars and stations you register.
  • Approximate location (town): your town/city, derived from your connection's IP via a geolocation service (city level, with coordinates rounded), used only to show local weather alongside your cat's activity. We never store your precise location.
  • A record of your consent (that you agreed, and the policy version).
  • One strictly-necessary cookie holding your login token, so the dashboard knows you're signed in. No advertising or tracking cookies.

The collar's microphone is used only for short, on-device classification (for example eat vs drink). Raw audio is processed on the device and discarded, it is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

3. Why we use it, and our lawful basis

We use this data only to provide the service: to show you your cat's activity on your dashboard. Our lawful basis is your consent (given at sign-up) and the performance of the service you asked for. We do not use it for advertising and we never sell it.

4. Who can see it

Your data is private to your account. Other users cannot see it; database rules scope every read and write to your own account. It is stored on our behalf by Google Firebase (our data processor). The only exception is the public demo account, which shows the maintainer's own cat for demonstration.

5. How long we keep it

We keep your data until you delete it. You can erase everything at any time from your account page ("Delete my account and data").

6. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access your data , use "Download my data" on your account page.
  • Portability , that download is machine-readable JSON.
  • Erasure , use "Delete my account and data".
  • Rectification , correct names and settings in the app, or contact us.
  • Withdraw consent , stop using the service and delete your account.
  • Complain , to the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk).

7. Security

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS), access is restricted per account by server-side rules, and your devices carry only a scoped, revocable token, never your account password. See the project's security notes for detail.

8. Changes

If we change this policy we will update the date and version above and, for material changes, ask you to agree again.
