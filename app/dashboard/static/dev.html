Mode

Training (default): the station records and uploads labelled clips , use the capture cards below to collect data. Production: the station stops recording and uploading entirely and just relays the collar's on-device behaviour classification. Switch to Production once your models are trained and flashed.

Actions to recognise

The behaviours your collar learns , eat, drink, resting, moving, scratch… anything you can capture and tell apart. These fill the label dropdown on each clip and the buttons below, and the trainer learns whatever set you define here.

Record an action (live label)

Press the button for what your cat is doing right now. The station records for the chosen length at any distance (ignoring the range gate), and every clip is auto-labelled with that action , no listening and labelling afterwards. Use this for behaviours away from the bowl: resting, sleeping, walking, playing, purring. The buttons come from your action set above.

Bowl capture (label later)

For behaviours at the bowl (eat, drink): when ON, the station records clips back-to-back whenever a registered collar is within the range set below. Label these afterwards from the audio in Recorded clips. Turn it OFF when you're done. (Training mode only , ignored in Production.)

How close the cat must be before the station records. Stronger / less-negative = must be closer. Measure the value with the cat at the bowl, then set a few dB below it.

Recorded clips

Clips your station captured, grouped by day then recording session. Each row shows its label, length and whether it has paired motion data. Set the action from the dropdown, or click a row to expand it and load the waveform: drag the start / end markers to cut dead space, Play to preview, then Save trim. The trim is a non-destructive window, applied to the audio and the time-aligned motion together. Delete the clips that aren't good for training. Only expanded clips load their audio, so a long list stays fast.
