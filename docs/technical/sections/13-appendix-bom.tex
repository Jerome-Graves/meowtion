\section{Bill of materials}
\label{sec:appendix-bom}

The complete parts list for one collar and one station, plus the tools and print files. There is no
custom PCB; each device is a Seeed XIAO module in a 3D-printed shell. Source of truth:
\texttt{hardware/README.md}.

\begin{table}[ht]
  \centering
  \caption{Collar parts (one per cat).}
  \label{tab:bom-collar}
  \begin{tabularx}{\textwidth}{@{}lcX@{}}
    \toprule
    \textbf{Part} & \textbf{Qty} & \textbf{Notes} \\
    \midrule
    Seeed XIAO nRF52840 Sense & 1 & SoC with BLE; on-module IMU + PDM microphone; runs inference \\
    1S LiPo cell, 3.7\,V 100\,mAh, with protection (BMS) & 1 & Soldered to the BAT pads; protected cell required \\
    20\,mm hydrophobic PTFE filter membrane & 1 & Over the mic hole; passes sound, blocks water/dust \\
    10\,mm quick-release safety collar & 1 & Standard strap; the TPU skin mounts to it \\
    \bottomrule
  \end{tabularx}
\end{table}
