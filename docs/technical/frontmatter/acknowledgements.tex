\section*{Acknowledgements}

Meowtion was built for the \textbf{HackTheKitty} hackathon, and we thank its organisers, mentors and
judges for the theme, the deadline, and the excuse to put a microcontroller on a cat. The project is
the work of \textbf{Jerome Graves} and \textbf{Rose Delcour-Min}. It also stands on a large body of
open-source work, the Zephyr and ESP-IDF projects, TensorFlow Lite Micro and CMSIS-NN, Firebase and
Streamlit, and the wider embedded and machine-learning communities whose tools made a two-person build
of this scope possible.

Our greatest debt, though, is to \textbf{Rupert}, the cat. He wore every awkward prototype without
complaint, treated each new collar as a minor inconvenience between naps, and, simply by being himself,
supplied the ground truth this entire system is built on. Every eating, drinking, grooming and resting
example in these pages traces back to him. He remains entirely unimpressed.
